    Adam Putnam is an American politician of the Republican Party serving as Florida’s Commissioner of Agriculture and is currently running to be Florida’s Governor in the 2018 elections. I had the opportunity to work on his personal website which is a pivotal focal point in showcasing his campaign. My team and I develop a fully functional website, based from mockups given to us by the design department. My focuses were to build the splash, volunteer, and contact page on Adams website. Google Analytics were strongly used in the QA process in order to grow a substantial amount of new supporters.

For my final project at The Iron Yard, which was demoed to the public at Capital Factory, I created a computer game named Snack Attack in HTML 5 with falling object simulations of food, allowing players to use the left and right arrow key to maneuver the frying pan in order to “catch” as much food as possible in the time allotted. During the planning process, I created wireframes, which helped map out the functionality and user experience. The style treatments assisted in the creation of all background and food icons seen throughout the game. All illustration designs were made in Adobe illustrator.
